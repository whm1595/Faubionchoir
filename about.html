About Our Program

Discover what makes the Faubion Choir Family special.

Our Mission

The Faubion Choir program is dedicated to providing an enriching musical experience for every student. We believe that choral music builds confidence, fosters teamwork, and creates a sense of belonging. Our program is open to all students who want to explore the joy of singing.

A Tradition of Excellence

Faubion Middle School is part of McKinney Independent School District, which is recognized as having one of the most distinguished Fine Arts programs in the state of Texas.

🏆

TMEA Honor

McKinney ISD is the only district in Texas to have a TMEA Honor Choir at every level — elementary, middle school, and high school.

🎧

National Recognition

Named one of the Best Communities in the nation for music education in 2017 and 2018.

🌵

Community

Located in the heart of North Central Texas, McKinney ISD serves more than 24,500 students across multiple campuses.
