Our Directors

Meet the talented musicians who lead the Faubion Choir program.

Choir Leadership Team

♫

Bridgette Pineau

Head Choir Director

Ms. Pineau has been teaching middle school and high school choir for the past six years. She is a graduate of the University of North Texas, where she developed her passion for choral music education.

Under her leadership, the Faubion Choir program continues to foster a welcoming environment where students build confidence, develop musical skills, and create lasting friendships.

♬

Jim Wilson

Accompanist

Jim Wilson has been an active member of the Dallas–Fort Worth music community for the last thirty years. He currently serves as the pianist at Christ United Methodist Church in Plano, Texas, and is the full-time accompanist for the Children's Chorus of Collin County and the choral programs at Faubion Middle School, Plano Senior High School, Frisco Centennial High School, and several other schools.

Jim has served as the pianist for nationally recognized events and groups, including the 2018, 2019, 2020, and 2022 Texas All-State Choirs, the Dallas Chamber Choir, and the 2016–2019 Missouri State University Choral Institutes.

He plays keyboards for the band Boomtown, performs with several local jazz and cover bands, and works nationally as a pianist with vocalists and instrumentalists. Jim is a dedicated volunteer and child advocate with Compassion International, and actively works to support and advocate for Latin American neighbors in need, both here and abroad.
